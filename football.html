Football History

I am a football player at Willamette University and I play on the Offensive Line as the starting Left Guard. This year will be my third season at Willamette and my second season as a collegiate starter. It will also be my 16th year of football. I played football at Hamilton High School in Chandler, Arizona. My senior year, we were ranked number 13 and 23 on MaxPrep's two Top 25 lists for national football teams as well as number 16 on their top dynasty teams list.
